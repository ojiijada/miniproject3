The Family Business

Tamika Heard recalls how her mother and stepfather founded Makeda's Cookies when she was a young college student in 1999. While she and her family didn't get a glimpse of entrepreneurial spirits earlier in life, they certainly made up for it by bringing their cookie bakery from idea to fruition.

Butter cookies are a beloved tradition in southern school cafeterias. I can remember eating fresh, warm butter cookies as a child during lunchtime, and my own mother also has fond memories of the crunchy cookie. Makeda's Cookies has allowed many other Mid-Southerners to reminisce on past memories while eating a classic butter cookie.

Now, the family business boasts three locations in Memphis, with potential plans of expanding outside of the city soon.
