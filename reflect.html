Reflections

Conducting the audio interview as well as taking the pictures of Tamika Heard was a very rewarding experience as both a Memphian and a student journalist. Not only did I get to "get in the field" and practice conducting and editing an interview, but I also got the chance to sit down with someone who I often saw and spoke to when I frequented Makeda's Cookies.

Over the process of this assignment, I learned how to ask better questions, how to search for a potential interviewee, and I also learned more behind the face of Tamika Heard, a passionate and colorful Memphian who loves to help kids and better her community.
